About the Author

Chinua Eric Robinson is a systems architect and public intellectual focused on generational infrastructure within Black families. Born in the Bronx and raised across Harlem, Brooklyn, and Laurelton, Queens, he worked his way from low-wage labor to executive-level positions in security, finance, and data analytics—all while earning a BA in Psychology and a Master's in Industrial & Organizational Psychology. He is the father of two sons, ages 2 and 9. Through The 7 Billion Dollar Brain™ and Robinsonville Financial Network™, he builds systems that help families create continuity instead of constantly restarting.
